Why This Exists

Male friendship is in free fall. We're doing something about it.

Somewhere between careers, family, and screens, most men quietly lost their circle. The group chat isn't the same as the group. And every man knows it.

Men's Group is built on one conviction: men grow when they show up for each other, face to face, on a schedule. The app handles the logistics of finding your group, scheduling meetings, and tracking goals, so the room can do what only a room can do.

Our success metric isn't your screen time. It's whether you were in the room this week. The app is designed to get you off your phone and into your life.

How It Works

Three steps. Zero flaking.

01

Start your group

Gather the men you already know and give your group a home, or join one near you as new groups open. Church groups, gym crews, book clubs, veteran circles. Locked groups stay private to members.

02

Set your goals

Set personal goals across faith, fitness, career, family, and finances. Your goals are yours. In a future update, you will choose exactly which goals to share and who sees them.

03

Show up. Repeat.

RSVP, meet in person, and keep the streak alive. Real accountability means someone notices when you don't show, and calls you, with care, when you don't follow through.

Inside the App

Built for men who'd rather be there than scroll

  • Groups & Meetings

    Create or join groups, schedule recurring in-person meetings, RSVP in two taps, and see who's coming.

  • Personal Goals

    Set personal goals across the areas of life that matter. Sharing goals with your group is coming, and it will always be your choice.

  • Calendar & Reminders

    One calendar for everything your group does, so meetings actually happen.

  • The Nexus

    A job board and curated book recommendations, because a strong group opens doors and sharpens minds.
