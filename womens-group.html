Why This Exists

Community shouldn't be this hard to find

Between work, family, and everything in between, deep friendship is often the first thing to go quiet. Group texts scatter. Plans slip. And the women who would show up for you are out there. You just haven't been in the same room yet.

Women's Group is built on one conviction: women thrive in consistent, intentional community. The app handles the logistics of finding your circle, scheduling gatherings, and tracking your goals, so your time together can be about what actually matters: each other. Privacy stays in your hands: hosts control whether a circle is private or public, because feeling safe is the precondition for everything else.

Our success metric isn't your screen time. It's whether you were with your people this week. The app is designed to get you off your phone and into your life.

How It Works

Three steps to your people

01

Start your circle

Gather the women you already love and give your circle a home, or join one near you as new circles open. Faith circles, book clubs, wellness groups. Hosts choose whether a circle is private or open, so your space is exactly as protected as you want it to be.

02

Grow together

Set personal intentions across faith, wellness, career, family, and creativity. Your goals are yours. In a future update, you will choose exactly which goals to share and who sees them.

03

Show up. Belong.

RSVP, gather in person, and watch a group of women become your women. The kind who notice when you're missing, and check in when life gets heavy.

Inside the App

Designed to bring women closer in real life

  • Circles & Gatherings

    Create or join groups, schedule recurring get-togethers, RSVP in two taps, and see who's coming.

  • Personal Goals

    Set intentions across the areas of life that matter. Sharing goals with your circle is coming, and it will always be your choice.

  • Calendar & Reminders

    One calendar for everything your group does, so gatherings actually happen.

  • The Nexus

    A job board and curated book recommendations, because a strong circle opens doors and nourishes minds.
